House Trace Puzzle Game

Trace the right houses fast. Get better at spotting stuff quickly and try to beat your best time.

House Trace is basically a spot-the-house game where you're hunting through these cute neighborhood scenes. A target appears, you scan the picture, and you trace the correct house shape in one confident move. The faster you find it, the higher your score.

  • Fast "spot-and-trace" levels

  • Smooth tracing controls

  • Score depends on speed and accuracy
