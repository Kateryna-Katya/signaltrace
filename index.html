How the Puzzle Works

Each level gives you a clear target — a specific house type, style, or detail. You scan the neighborhood scene, spot the match, and trace it quickly. Pretty easy at first, but then it gets harder - they start making the houses look really similar: more similar rooftops, more distractions, and smaller clues hiding in plain sight.

STEP 01

Check the Target

Study the target house details — roof shape, windows, decorations.

STEP 02

Scan the Scene

Browse the neighborhood to find the matching house among look-alikes.

STEP 03

Trace the Outline

Draw your finger along the house outline smoothly and precisely.

STEP 04

Build a Streak

Chain correct traces to unlock score multipliers and bonuses.

A Cozy Neighborhood Packed with Clues

Each level is basically a little visual puzzle. Two houses may share the same roof color, but the window shape gives it away. Another match might hide behind decorations, lighting, or tiny architectural quirks. The more you play, the more your brain starts building a personal shortcut library of “tells” you can spot instantly.

  • Rooflines that almost match

  • Window patterns as the real giveaway

  • Small decor elements that become quick anchors

  • Bright, friendly visuals that still test focus
