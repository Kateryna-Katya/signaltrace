How the Puzzle Works

Each level gives you a clear target — a specific house type, style, or detail. You scan the neighborhood scene, spot the match, and trace it quickly. Pretty easy at first, but then it gets harder - they start making the houses look really similar: more similar rooftops, more distractions, and smaller clues hiding in plain sight.

STEP 01

Check the Target

Study the target house details — roof shape, windows, decorations.

STEP 02

Scan the Scene

Browse the neighborhood to find the matching house among look-alikes.

STEP 03

Trace the Outline

Draw your finger along the house outline smoothly and precisely.

STEP 04

Build a Streak

Chain correct traces to unlock score multipliers and bonuses.
